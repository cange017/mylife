GLUTEN-FREE 2 INGREDIENT PANCAKES

Ingredients:

Directions:

  1. Put banana in the bowl and smash it with the fork.

  2. Crack the egg, pour it over another bowl, and whish it with the fork.

  3. Pour the egg into the bowl with the banana and mix it together.

  4. **OPTIONAL: Add cinammon to the mixture for more flavor.

  5. Put the pan on the stove and add olive oil/coconut oil/vegetable oil for the pancakes to not stick to the pan.

  6. Turn the stove on high.

  7. Wait 2 minutes for pan to heat up and then pour the mixture into the pan, making sure that you make several pancakes.

  8. **OPTIONAL: Lower the heat a little and then add blueberries on top of the pancakes.

  9. Wait around 2 minutes or less and flip the pancakes over.

  10. Turn off the stove and voila!
