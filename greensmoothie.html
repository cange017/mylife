nutrient-packed green smoothie

Serving size: 1 person

Ingredients

  1. 1 banana (frozen preferably)

  2. 2 big handfuls of spinach

  3. 1 cup of milk of your choice

  4. 1 teaspoon of flax or chia seeds

  5. 1/3 juice of lemon

  6. 1/2 teaspoon of cinnamon

  7. OPTIONAL: small dash of cayenne pepper (it kickstarts your metabolism, but do not add too much because it is spicy!)

Directions

  1. Peel and cut one banana into pieces and store in ziplock bag overnight

  2. Get all of the ingredients and add them in the blender to blend.

  3. Done!

Enjoy!

Notes/Disclaimers

Use more or less milk for a thicker or thinner smoothie.
