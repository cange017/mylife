creamy overnight oats

Serving size: 1 person

Ingredients

  1. Oatmeal

  2. Banana or other fruit

  3. Milk of your choice

  4. Cinammon

Directions

  1. Cook the oats in the microwave or in the stove

    • Bring the water to a boil with 3-2 water to oat ratio.
    • Add the oats once the water boils (when you see bubbles) and drop the temperature to a little above medium.
    • Stir the oats for three minutes.
    • Cover and rest for 5 minutes.

  2. Once the oatmeal is ready, put them in a container to keep overnight in the fridge

  3. The next morning, take them oat and serve yourself around a cup of the oats

  4. Pour a third cup of milk into the bowl

  5. Cut the banana into slices and add it to the bowl

  6. **OPTIONAL: Add a spoon of cinammon to the bowl for more flavor.

  7. **OPTIONAL: Add a spoon of nut butter, maple syrup, or honey for a sweeter taste.

Enjoy!

Notes/Disclaimers

While some peole may prefer to microwave their oats or make just enough for one serving, I truly recommend that you make a week's serving of oatmeal instead. There is nothing better than having a meal ready to go!
